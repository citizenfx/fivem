## SET_MILLISECONDS_PER_GAME_MINUTE

```c
void SET_MILLISECONDS_PER_GAME_MINUTE(int value);
```

Overrides how many real ms are equal to one game minute.
A setter for [`GetMillisecondsPerGameMinute`](#_0x2F8B4D1C595B11DB).

## Parameters
* **value**: Milliseconds.
